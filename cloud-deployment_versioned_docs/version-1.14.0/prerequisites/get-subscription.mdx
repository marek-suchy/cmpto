Live Transcoder operates as a subscription-based software delivered via Docker image. To obtain access, contact the Comprimato support team.

## Obtaining Your Subscription

The support specialists will provision your subscription and grant you access to the Customer Dashboard at [transcoder.comprimato.com](https://transcoder.comprimato.com).

## What You'll Receive

The Customer Dashboard provides three essential items:

1. **Personal access token** — Used to authenticate with the private DockerHub repository. Format: `dckr_pat_` followed by alphanumeric characters (legacy tokens use UUID format).

2. **Docker Compose file** — A pre-configured `docker-compose.yml` with your embedded license key.

3. **License key** — A unique identifier in UUID format, for example:
   ```
   64544bd5-8df6-490c-bce7-468d035a8b1a
   ```

## Next Steps

While awaiting your subscription credentials, you can prepare your deployment environment. Setup guides are available for:

- Amazon Web Services (AWS)
- Google Cloud Platform
- Oracle Cloud Infrastructure
- Microsoft Azure
- On-premises servers

Once your subscription is ready, you'll be equipped to deploy the Docker container with your provided credentials.
